Spot Fraud

Recognize common signs of fraud to stop scams before they happen.

Urgency Tactics

  • “Act now” or “immediate action required” demands.
  • Threats of arrest, legal action, or account closure if you don’t respond right away.
  • Pressure to make a payment or share information quickly.

Payment Red Flags

  • Requests for payment by gift cards.
  • Wire transfers to unknown individuals or companies.
  • Demands for cryptocurrency payments.
  • Sending money through payment apps to strangers.

Impersonation Signs

  • Emails or calls pretending to be from government agencies.
  • Contact claiming to be your bank or a reputable company asking for personal information.
  • Unsolicited tech support offering to fix nonexistent problems.
