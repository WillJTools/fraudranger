Report Fraud

Follow the links below to report fraud directly to the appropriate federal agency. Fraud Ranger does not collect your reports or personal information.

Identity Theft & General Scams

Report identity theft, phone scams, charity scams, and other general fraud.

Federal Trade Commission – reportfraud.ftc.gov

Internet & Cyber Crime

Report online scams, phishing emails, cyber‑enabled fraud, and other internet crimes.

FBI Internet Crime Complaint Center – ic3.gov

Investment Fraud

Report Ponzi schemes, unregistered securities, and other investment fraud.

U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission – sec.gov/tcr

Tax Fraud

Report false tax filings, identity theft related to taxes, or impersonation of IRS officials.

Internal Revenue Service – irs.gov

Social Security Fraud

Report misuse of Social Security numbers or benefits.

Social Security Administration, Office of the Inspector General – oig.ssa.gov

Financial Institution Complaints

Report issues with banks, credit cards, or other financial products.

Consumer Financial Protection Bureau – consumerfinance.gov/complaint
