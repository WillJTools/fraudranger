About Fraud Ranger

Fraud Ranger is an independent public service guide created to help U.S. residents quickly identify and respond to fraud. It is not a government agency and does not collect or store personal information.

This website was designed to provide clear, authoritative guidance. Our goal is to simplify the process of understanding what happened, knowing what to do first, reporting fraud to the appropriate federal agencies, and recovering afterward.

Disclaimer

Fraud Ranger provides general information intended to help victims of fraud. We do not provide legal advice, and nothing on this site should be interpreted as such. For specific guidance, please consult an attorney or the appropriate authorities.

We strive to keep information up to date but cannot guarantee accuracy. Always follow instructions provided by official government agencies.
